Who Is GRAY Music Factory?

Two young musician-songwriters met in 1978 and quickly produced a vast portfolio of original music. Their music soon got the attention of major record labels in LA (Arista, Capital, and EMI), but they ultimately choose family and careers over the uncertainty of a fickle industry. Through it all, their love of writing music continued to burn within and the songwriting continued.

During those (wilderness) years they met other musicians with similar stories. Slowly and inexorably, GRAY began to form. Now they return, older and wiser, to pursue the musical journey they began so many years ago.
